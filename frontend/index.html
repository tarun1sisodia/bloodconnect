How It Works

BloodConnect makes it easy to donate blood or find donors when you need them most.

Register

Create an account and specify if you want to be a donor. Add your blood type and location.

Find or Request

Search for donors in your area or create a blood request for yourself or someone else.

Connect

Connect with donors or recipients and coordinate the blood donation process.
